# CRANK CITY — Full Storyline

A neon-noir crime story for Playdate. One getaway driver, one city, one debt
that turns into a war against the company that owns everything.

## Premise

**MERIDIAN** is a harbor city wired end to end by **HELIX**, a "security"
conglomerate that put every lock, car, camera, and bank account online — and
watches all of it. Anything networked can be seen, logged, and seized. So the
people Helix squeezed went analog: hand-cranked safes, dumb cars, paper, dials.
No signal, no logs, nothing to subpoena. In Meridian, **going analog is the only
way to move unseen** — and the crank is how you do it.

You are **RYE**, the best wheelman in Meridian, deep in debt to a man who owns
your time.

## Cast

- **RYE** — protagonist. Cool, dry, loyal to the few people who earned it. Drives
  anything, cracks any safe Elena taught him, and never panics behind the wheel.
- **VIKTOR** — old-school boss. Calls in Rye's debt. Brutal, fair in his own way,
  hiding that Helix already has a hook in him.
- **NYX** — crew leader from outside Viktor's world. Sharp, untouchable, runs the
  dock jobs. Says she's "the reason you get paid." Knows more than she admits.
- **MARA** — salvage diver, ex-Helix marine-ops. Took Rye under the water once and
  he never forgot it. Carries guilt for a ship she helped sink.
- **ELENA** — the master who taught Rye the dial. Retired, hands shaking, pulled
  back in for the one box that matters.

## The spine

Helix buried evidence of what it did to Meridian — the **LEDGER** — split into
three encrypted fragments hidden where only analog hands can reach them: a vault,
a sunken ship, and Helix's own tower. Recover all three and the city can finally
move against Helix at dawn. Everyone wants the Ledger. Almost no one can be
trusted with it.

## Three acts, twelve missions

### ACT I — THE DEBT (you don't choose this life, it chooses you)
1. **Cold Start** *(drive)* — Viktor calls in the debt. Get to the garage unseen.
2. **Open It** *(safe/crank)* — crack Viktor's old box. Inside: a note that proves
   Helix is leaning on him.
3. **Blueline** *(chase)* — a Helix alarm trips. Outrun BLUELINE, Helix's private
   police, to the hideout. Viktor decides Rye is worth keeping.

### ACT II — THE PORT (the job gets bigger than the man who gave it)
4. **The Docks** *(foot/port)* — Nyx appears. Pull four crates off the *Argent's*
   manifest before patrols sweep the docks.
5. **Below the Argent** *(sonar)* — Mara takes Rye under. Crank the sonar in the
   dark, recover the crate that went down with the ship.
6. **Cross Town** *(drive)* — run the goods uptown to the buyer. Rye's gut says
   keep the engine running.
7. **The Buyer** *(safe/crank)* — crack the buyer's vault for payment. It isn't
   cash — it's **Helix ledgers**. Nyx set the crew up to *steal evidence.*

### ACT III — THE LEDGER (everyone wants it; trust almost no one)
8. **Burned** *(chase)* — the city lights up. Every badge wants the fragment. Run.
9. **Find Nyx** *(foot)* — track her through the old market. The truth: she's
   ex-Helix too, building a case from the inside. **Ledger Fragment 1** is real.
10. **The Deep Vault** *(sonar)* — Mara dives Rye past the *Argent* to a data pod,
    bad air all the way down. **Fragment 2.** Mara settles her ghost.
11. **Helix Tower** *(safe/crank)* — Elena walks Rye through the hardest box ever
    built, inside Helix's own tower. Hands steady. **Fragment 3.**
12. **Dawn Run** *(chase, finale)* — carry the full Ledger to the boat before
    sunrise, max heat, Blueline everywhere. Make it, and Meridian goes dark on
    Helix at dawn.

## The twist & the ending

Viktor was Helix's hook from the start — the debt was bait to keep Rye close so
Helix could find the crew sniffing the Ledger. When it counts, Viktor chooses the
city over his handler and buys Rye the seconds he needs on the Dawn Run. Rye gets
the Ledger to Nyx's boat. Sun comes up. Helix goes dark. Last line, Rye to Nyx:
*"We really made it." —"We? Careful, wheelman. That almost sounded like a crew."*

## How the gameplay carries the story

Every mechanic is a story beat, never filler:
- **Crank-safe** = Rye's craft and Elena's legacy — the three fragment vaults.
- **Sonar dive** = Mara's guilt and the buried *Argent* — choosing to see in the dark.
- **Driving/chase** = Rye's whole identity, and Blueline tightening the net.
- **On-foot/port** = the human, exposed moments between the loud ones.

## Cutscenes

Delivered in the dialogue system (portrait + nameplate + typewriter), with the
characters **animated** — they bob and "talk" while their line types out, blink
of motion between beats. Built from your DEADBLOCK cast, dithered to 1-bit:
Rye, Viktor, Nyx, Mara, Elena.
